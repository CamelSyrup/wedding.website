FAQs

What time should we arrive?

You are welcome to arrive from 1pm. We kindly ask that you arrive no later than 1:30pm, as the ceremony will begin at 2pm. If you arrive after the ceremony begins, the venue staff will ask you to wait in the bar area until the ceremony is over.

What is the dress code?

Formal, black tie optional. We encourage anyone considering a bow tie to go for it!

We kindly ask that you avoid the colours blue and white, as these colours are reserved for the bridal party.

How do I RSVP?

Really...!? We kindly ask that you RSVP before 30th November 2024 so that we are able to plan accordingly. If you RSVP and then are subsequently unable to attend, please let us know as soon as possible.

Can I bring a plus one?

Due to venue limits on capacity, we can only accomodate guests named on the envelope.

Is there an arrangement for transport to and from the venue?

To enable maximum flexibility for everyone, we have not organised any group transport. There are a number of taxi companies in the area, which can be found below. We have also used Uber in the area successfully. Most people will be staying in Cheltenham so group taxis can be arranged if needed. There is also parking available on-site.

Starline Taxis: 01242 250250
Dial A Cab: 01242 242424
Reliance Taxis: 01285 640950
Andy's Cars: 01452 523000
Pulhams Coaches: 01451 820369

Please note that the venue has very limited phone signal and WiFi so we strongly encourage you to make transport arrangements beforehand. The venue has a landline phone to book last-minute taxis if plans change.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

We have decided to have an unplugged ceremony - please do not take any photos during the ceremony or confetti throw. We invite you to be fully present with us and let our photographer do the work!

What time will the reception finish?

The bar and music will finish at midnight and the site will close at 1am.

Are there any pre-wedding events?

We are planning to have a very informal welcome drinks at a pub in Cheltenham on Thursday 14th August. We will let you have more details closer to the time!
