FAQs

What is the dress code?

The dress code is formal. We encourage anyone considering a bow tie to go for it!

Are children invited?

No, we've got an 18+ rule across the guest list and venue.

Can I bring a plus one?

No, we have a restrictive limit at the venue, therefore, are unable to accommodate any guests not named on the invites.

What time should we arrive?

We ask that you arrive no later than 1:30pm as the ceremony starts at 2pm.

How do I RSVP?

Really!? But on that note, please RSVP before 31st October 2024.

Is there an arrangement for transport to and from the venue?

No, everyone needs to organise their own transport.

There are a number of taxi companies in the area, which can be found here.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

If you value your life, make sure that Syd doesn't see you.

What should I do if I need to cancel last minute?

Firstly, we hope everything is okay! Secondly, just let us know as soon as possible on WhatsApp and submit a new RSVP entry selecting 'No'.

Will there be vegetarian / vegan / gluten-free menu options?

Not options, but let us know ASAP and we can co-ordinate with the caterers so that you get the relevant dietry adjustments on your meal.
